Annie Easley

Mathematician, Computer Scientist, Hidden Figure

(1933 - 2011)

Easley's Accomplishments

  • Hired at NASA as a "Human Computer" when it was still NACA in 1955
  • Helped develop and launch the Centaur rocket
  • Served as equal employment opportunity counselor in addition to her role as a computer engineer
  • Received a bachelor's in mathematics while working full time at NASA
  • Researched and developed early battery designs for hybrid energy vehicles during the 70's energy crisis
  • Created a ski club and served as president to boost morale
  • Worked on project examining holes in the Earth's ozone layer
  • Helped recruit young people of color and women to work for NASA
  • Worked at NASA for 35 years
